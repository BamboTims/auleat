How it works

The food ordering website can be accessed from any device with internet access (desktop computers, laptops, tablets, etc.).

01 Select Meals

To place an order, simply click the “Add to Cart” button next to the item you want. You can also add multiple items to your order in a single click.

02 Make Payment

You’ll enter your billing and shipping information and choose a payment method.

03 Come for Pickup

Get Your order number and your meal will be ready for pickup when you arrive.
